Recommendations for Technical Support Requests

When reporting bugs or file conversion issues, sharing the following information will help us diagnose and resolve your issue much faster:

  • Your Operating System (OS) (e.g., Windows 11, macOS Sequoia, etc.)
  • Your Web Browser name and version (e.g., Google Chrome v125, etc.)
  • The original file format and option settings that triggered the issue
  • Error logs from your browser's developer console (F12) if available

Your suggestions and reports are crucial for making our tools better. We appreciate your support.
